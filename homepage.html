Project 32 Online Courses

Welcome to our Project Page, which will discuss everything that has been going on about online courses.

Online courses have now made a very huge impact on our everyday life, playing a very important role in the education industry all over the world ,helping people to attain knowledge in the way that has never been done berfore.

Online courses have changed the way we view education and the method we educate ourselves completely, for sure. But there's a huge long line of its history and its development through as time passed by. Along with its emergence and its spread, online learning also leads to controversial and opposite notions and views, engendering heated debates among the educators, the students and the society as well. Well. let's dig a bit deeper into various aspects about online learning and lets see its prospects in the context of Vietnamese culture and economy.
