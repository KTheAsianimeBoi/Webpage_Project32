The development of online course in Vietnam and its prospects

Development

Vietnam has been very fast to catch up with the trend of online studying with the whole world. The method of online studying was just emerging and beggining to get its ground in 2010 around the world; and right at that time, a lot of domestic enterprises started to establish a multitude of pages like Hocmai.vn,Topica,Onluyen.vn,Speakup.vn, Mathplay...

Online courses in Vietnam mainly revolves around teaching English and speaking English, preparation for test especially for the students below university-level and skills teaching , varying from graphic design to computer programming to cooking..

Prospects

Vietnam is a highly potential environment for online learning thanks to the fact that more than 60% of the population have access to the Internet. Most of the users are young people who have high desire and yearning to learn and are ready to pay for the courses.


The investment rate on the e-learning industrials into Vietnam is also really huge . In 2018, Northstar Singapore from Singapore invested wobbling 50 million USD into Topica Edtech Group. Edu2Review, an online platform for registering online courses and giving reviews to different institutions in Vietnam, also received a fair amount of money and now it is deemed to be worth million of dollars, which is five times the amount it was in 2017.

The method of e-learning is also the aim and the pathway that is being implemented by the Minustry of Education and Learning. In particular, many univerisities have generalized the usage of e-learning. The Ho Chi Minh University of Technology is also an avid supporter for this learning method.
