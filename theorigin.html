Why do we need online courses?

1.Geographical distance

The world is a vast place. Why should you limit your knowledge to just people around you? You can study from people who are half the world away from you? This solves the problem of distance traveling posed by offline studying, which is often the case for people in rural areas.

2.Personal Needs

Well, studying is not the only thing that matters to your life. If you have a plan for yourself, you can esily organize it into your schedule and rearrange your studying session some time else.


Or maybe you are stuck with some business that you don't feel like studying right at the moment. Or maybe you just experienced somthing sad and you don't want to study because you have no will to absorb anything new.

3. Time

As the internet and its service gets more and more developed, many people started to feel tired with restricted schedule and they lean towards studying online, which is extremely flexible in terms of time . Now you don't have to wake up early or have to bear the schedule in your mind because it is all up to you to decide what time in the day you want to study .

4. Widespread illness

And yes, online studying thrives the most especially in time like these! The last year of 2020 hightlights the utmost importance of online courses to the entire world. Had it not been for the online studying method, the world would have suffered so much in terms of development and schools as well as universities around the world would be dragged down a lot.
